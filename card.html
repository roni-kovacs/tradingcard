Elmo the dog

Elmo was a stray and liked to run away. He is soft and sweet but also full of greed, very loving but not so neat.

  • Breed: Kooikerhondje
  • Age: Around 1.5 years old
  • Habitat: He likes the nature but prefers a soft cushion in the house
  • Food: Highest quality dog food and pizza apparantely

  • Distinctive mark: Falls asleep in the weirdest positions

He is a one of a kind dog, likes humans and other dogs and has very soft fur around his neck. It is rather difficult to earn his trust, he loves to snuggle though. Will fall asleep with his head hanging out of his dog bed. He doesn't really listen to his family, only when it pleases him.

Overall 14/10 though, the best boy.
